Part 2 - Year of the Four Emperors: 

Galba was still in Spain when he received the news he had become emperor. 
He took at least a month to secure the Spanish provinces before leaving. 
He appointed Cluvius Rufus as his replacement in his own province, 
but also murdered several of his opponents there, including Obultronius Sabinus, 
the probable governor of Baetica.
