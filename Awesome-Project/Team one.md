Group Scrum project on small town beisde the factory which makes ecological fuel from microplastic gathered from the ocean.

First we chose our Scrum leader and Product owner, and then created product backlog from product owner’s user stories. The Backlog included following items:

1.Main factory (ecological fuel)  
2.Housings for employees<br/> 
3.Grocery store<br/>
4.Pharmacy<br/> 
5.Healthcare<br/> 
6.Daycare<br/> 
7.Sport center<br/> 
8.Parking spot<br/> 
9.Emergency services<br/> 

Second we made our Sprint plan, whick includes followings:

1.Factory must be closer to ocean or docking station so that waste gathered from the ocean can be transported to the factory easily. 
2.The factory is going to be built with sandwich panels because it's faster, easier to build and cheaper. 
3.The factory will have 2 different spots for taking the waste. One from trucks and one from boats. 
4.The factory will have a locker room at the entrance of the factory, and on top of the locker room will be our kitchen.  
5.Factory better be in walking distance from the Houses.
6.Trees will be planted by the side of the roads so that smell from the factory cant be reached to the Houses.

And Finally we made a layout plan that inclutes items from the backlog and drew the plan on paper
