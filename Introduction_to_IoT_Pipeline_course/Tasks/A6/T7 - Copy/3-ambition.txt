Part 3 - Year of the Four Emperors: 

Otho was not keen to begin another civil war and sent emissaries to propose 
a peace and convey his offer to marry Vitellius's daughter. It was too late to reason; 
Vitellius's generals were leading half of his army toward Italy. After a series of minor 
victories, Otho suffered defeat in the First Battle of Bedriacum.
